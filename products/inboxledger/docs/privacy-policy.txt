# Privacy Policy

## Last Updated: January 20, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how the Expense Tracker Sheets Add-on ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Google Workspace add-on.

## Information We Collect

### Data You Provide
- Expense data you enter into Google Sheets
- Google account information (managed by Google)

### Automatically Collected Data
- Usage analytics (anonymized)
- Error logs for troubleshooting

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Provide and maintain the add-on functionality
- Improve user experience and features
- Ensure security and prevent abuse
- Comply with legal obligations

## Data Storage and Security

- All data remains in your Google account
- We do not store personal data on external servers
- Data is protected by Google's security measures
- You can delete all data by uninstalling the add-on

## Third-Party Services

This add-on integrates with:
- Google Workspace (Sheets, Drive)
- Google Analytics (usage statistics only)

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your data
- Delete your data
- Withdraw consent
- File complaints
