About

InboxLedger transforms Google Sheets into a powerful expense management tool. With automated categorization, intelligent data validation, and real-time expense summaries, tracking business and personal expenses becomes effortless and accurate.

Built using Google Apps Script and modern JavaScript, this add-on integrates seamlessly with Google Workspace. Perfect for freelancers, small businesses, and individuals who want professional expense tracking without complex software.
